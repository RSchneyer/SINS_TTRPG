# Characters

*Chargen: Character Generation*

4 core attributes + 1 chosen at chargen

- Strength (STR)
- Dexterity (DEX)
- Intelligence (INT)
- Charisma (CHA)

Then choose one of the following:
- Chrome (CHR)
- Magic (MAG)

## Chargen Choices
- CHR vs MAG
    - Allows for picking cybernetics or spells
- Metatype
    - Human, Elf, Dwarf, Orc, Troll
- Role
    - Street Sam, Decker, Face, Rigger
- Traits
    - 3 pools:
        - MAG prereq
        - CHR prereq
        - No prereq
